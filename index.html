For Customers

Four steps. Done.

From posting to delivery. No calls. No waiting on a stranger to answer a WhatsApp. Your job posted, moving, and tracked in minutes.

01
📍
Post Your Job

Enter pickup and dropoff. Describe your items or point the camera and let our AI describe them. An AI-generated price guide shows you the expected range before anyone bids.

02
⚡
Watch Bids Arrive

Nearby verified drivers see your job and submit bids in real time. The platform ranks bids by price, rating, distance, and job history so you can compare at a glance.

03
👁
Choose Your Driver

Review each driver's name, vehicle, star rating, completed jobs, and bid price. You decide who moves your goods. Payment is held securely until delivery is confirmed.

04
📷
Track and Confirm

Watch your driver's live GPS position throughout the job. A timestamped photo is taken at delivery. You confirm receipt and rate your driver. Payment releases automatically.

Why HALA

Not a WhatsApp group
with a logo.

Every feature exists because customers were let down and drivers were underpaid. We built the infrastructure that fixes both sides of that problem.

🛡
Identity-Verified Drivers

Every driver's South African ID is verified and every vehicle undergoes inspection before they can receive a single job on the platform.

📍
Live GPS Tracking

See your driver's live position on the map from the moment they accept to the moment they deliver. No guessing, no calling to ask where they are.

🤖
AI Price Guide

Before any driver bids, our AI calculates an expected price range based on distance, item type, floors, and current demand. You go in informed.

📷
Photo Proof of Delivery

Drivers take a timestamped photo at delivery and it uploads to the cloud against your job record. Any dispute has documented evidence.

💳
Escrow Payments

Payment is held securely by PayFast after you confirm a driver. It releases to the driver only after you confirm delivery. Your money does not move until the job is done.

🌿
Carbon Tracking

Every delivery's CO2 footprint is calculated per job. Carbon offset available at R20 per job. Full ESG reporting for corporate clients who need it.
